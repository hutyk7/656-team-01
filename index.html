We follow our special construction process:

Before starting the construction of your future home, we create a detailed design project.

Get it for free →

01. Figuring out your needs

We schedule a one-on-one appointment to help understand what you want to build. We have different ready-made projects of cottages.

02. Designing your dream cottage

Once we've figured out the requirements, we move on to the design process, creating preliminary drawings, home and build order drawings, and so on.

03. Moving on to the construction

When everything is designed and approved by you, the construction starts. We have a dedicated team of experienced constructors and engineers.

04. Checking the progress

We invite you to take a look at your future cottage and to suggest any changes and improvements that could be made at that stage of the process.

05. Adding final touches

We make final improvements and add finishing touches to give your cottage a more refined look. The final version of your cottage will look fantastic!

06. Giving you the keys

When we finish, you check the work once again and receive the keys to your brand new dream home! We congratulate you with your new place or residence!
